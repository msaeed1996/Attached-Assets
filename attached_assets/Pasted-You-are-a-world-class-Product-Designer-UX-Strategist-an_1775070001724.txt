I am building **TrueGigs.com**, a temporary staffing platform that connects **businesses (employers)** with **temporary workers (gig workers)** for short-term jobs.

Your job is to **completely rethink, redesign, and optimize the entire product experience from 0 → 1**, as if you were founding designer of a $100M startup.

# 🎯 CORE OBJECTIVE:

Design a **frictionless, trust-first, conversion-optimized platform** that:

* Outperforms competitors in **speed, clarity, and usability**
